This section covers major laws you'll encounter on the SAFE MLO Exam:

TILA (Truth in Lending Act)

  • Purpose: Promote the informed use of consumer credit
  • Requires disclosure of APR, finance charges, and terms
  • Right of Rescission: 3 business days for refinances on primary residence
  • Governs advertising with "trigger terms"

RESPA (Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act)

  • Purpose: Help consumers understand settlement costs and prevent kickbacks
  • Key Sections: Section 8 (Kickbacks), Section 10 (Escrow limits)
  • Disclosures: Loan Estimate (within 3 business days), Closing Disclosure
  • Requires Home Loan Toolkit on purchase transactions

ECOA (Equal Credit Opportunity Act)

  • Prohibits discrimination in credit based on race, color, religion, sex, marital status, etc.
  • Notice of Action required within 30 days
  • Requires explanation for credit denial

Other Key Laws

  • HMDA: Prevents redlining, requires demographic reporting
  • FCRA / FACTA: Credit report accuracy and identity theft prevention
  • GLBA: Safeguards Rule for protecting consumer financial info
  • MAP Rule: Prohibits misleading mortgage advertising

Disclosure Delivery Timeline

  • Loan Estimate (LE): Within 3 business days of application
  • Closing Disclosure (CD): 3 business days before consummation
  • AFBA Disclosure: At time of referral
  • Home Loan Toolkit: Within 3 business days (purchase only)
